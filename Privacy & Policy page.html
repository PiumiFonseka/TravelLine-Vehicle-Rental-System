Privacy and Policy





We place a high priority on the security and privacy of our customers' personal data at our vehicle rental website. We only gather the information we require, such as name, address, contact information, and driver's license details, in order to process bookings and complete transactions. We take strong precautions to protect this data against unauthorized access and disclosure. We only keep a customer's information for as long as is required before safely discarding it. With the sole purpose of providing services, personal information may be shared with reliable third-party service providers. Customers can withdraw their permission for the processing of their data and have access to and rectify their personal information. Cookies may be used on our website to improve user experience. We notify users of any updates to our privacy policy and maintain it current.


  1. Data Gathering and Use:

    2. a.Personal Data: The vehicle rental system will ask clients for personal data including name, address, phone number, and driver's license information. To process reservations, perform transactions, and adhere to legal regulations, this information is required.

    b.Usage Data: To enhance services, tailor products, and perform statistical analysis, the system may also collect usage data, such as rental history, vehicle preferences, and transaction details.



  2. Data Protection and Security:


    3. a.Confidentiality:The vehicle rental system shall treat all personal information as confidential and take the appropriate precautions to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration of data.

    b. Security Measures: To guard against loss, theft, unauthorized access, or misuse of customer data, the system will utilize the proper organizational and technical security measures. Access restrictions, firewalls, and regular security audits may be part of this.



  3. Data Retention:


    4. a. Data Retention: The vehicle rental system will keep customer information for as long as it takes to accomplish the goals listed in the privacy policy or as long as it is required by law.
    b. Data Disposal: The system will securely delete or anonymize personal data when it is no longer needed to avoid unauthorized usage.



  4. Third-Party Sharing:


    5. a. Limited Sharing:The vehicle rental system may share limited amounts of personal data with partners and third-party service providers who help offer services, such as vehicle suppliers, insurance companies, and payment processors. These organizations are required to safeguard consumer information and to use it only for certain reasons. b. Legal Requirements: The system may disclose personal data in order to defend its rights, property, or the safety of users if forced to do so by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

  5. User Rights:


    6. a. Access and Correction:Customers have the right to see, alter, or rectify the personal information about them that the car rental system has on file. Consent Withdrawal :Customers have the option to revoke their permission for the collection and use of their personal information. However, this can make it harder for the system to deliver services.



  6. Cookies and Tracking:


    7. a. Cookies: To improve user experience, track preferences, and compile data on website usage, the car rental system may make use of cookies or other similar technologies. Customers can change or deactivate cookies in their browser settings.



  7. Updates to Privacy Policy:


    8. a. Notification: Customers will be informed of any privacy policy changes by the car rental system through the relevant channels, such as online alerts or email. It is the client's duty to periodically evaluate the revised policy.
